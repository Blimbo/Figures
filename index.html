On this page you can pick up three dots (by clicking on the screen) and browser will build two figures:
  1. parallelogram - based on selected dots
  2. circle - with same square and center, as parallelogram has

If you want to clear drawing area, press the "reset" button.
Also you can move dots by click and drag them.
